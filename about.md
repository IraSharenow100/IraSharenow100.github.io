## About Me

I am Ira Sharenow, a data analyst based in El Cerrito, California. I hold an
M.A. in Mathematics and an M.S. in Business from the University of
Wisconsin-Madison, with graduate training in probability and stochastic processes.

My work focuses on housing markets, public finance, and local government data.
I analyze large real-world datasets and present findings through clear written
reports and visualizations. My primary portfolio project is a 30-year dataset
of single-family home sales in Madison, Wisconsin — 134,000+ transactions and
48 variables — which I analyze using R and Python.

I also apply data analysis to civic policy questions in El Cerrito, including
city budget trends, pension obligations, public safety performance, and the
proposed Measure C library project.
