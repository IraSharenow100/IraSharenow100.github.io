## Madison Housing Analysis

Thirty years of single-family home sales data for Madison, Wisconsin — 134,000+ transactions, 48 variables. This is my primary portfolio project.

**[Report 1: City of Madison (1994–2024)](/madison-home-price-appreciation/)**
Median and mean price trends, price per square foot, market volume, and home size over three decades.

**[Report 2: Madison Area including Suburbs (1994–2024)](/madison-area-report2/)**
Suburban price premiums, size differences, and long-term appreciation compared to the city.

## El Cerrito Public Finance and Safety

Data-driven analysis of El Cerrito, California — city finances, pension obligations, and public safety performance.

**[CalPERS Pension Liability Analysis](/calpers-ual-analysis/)**
El Cerrito's unfunded pension liability has reached crisis levels, crowding out basic city services.

**[City Budget Analysis](/el-cerrito-budget-analysis/)**
Structural deficits, rising pension costs, declining reserves, and long-term fiscal trends.

**[Fire Department Incident Analysis](/fire-department-analysis/)**
Response times, incident counts, and mutual aid patterns across El Cerrito, Kensington, and Richmond.

**[Police Incident Analysis (2019–2025)](/police-incident-analysis/)**
Citywide incident patterns, geographic heat maps, and trends over six years.
